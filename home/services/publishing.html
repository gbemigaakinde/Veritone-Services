Book Publishing Services

Full-service publishing to bring your book to life and into the hands of readers worldwide.

What is Book Publishing?

Book publishing involves taking your manuscript from draft to finished product, ready for sale and distribution. We handle all aspects: professional formatting, stunning cover design, ISBN acquisition, print-on-demand setup, eBook conversion, and global distribution through platforms like Amazon KDP, Okadabooks, and Ingram.

Ideal for authors seeking self-publishing excellence without the hassle of technical details—whether fiction, non-fiction, memoir, business book, or poetry.

Professional Presentation

High-quality covers, formatting, and production that compete with traditional publishers.

Global Distribution

Reach readers worldwide through major platforms, including print and digital.

Control & Royalties

Retain full rights and earn higher royalties with print-on-demand and self-publishing.

Our Detailed Book Publishing Process

1

Manuscript Review & Consultation

We start with a thorough review of your manuscript to assess readiness for publishing. We discuss your goals (e.g., print, eBook, audiobook), target audience, and vision. This consultation includes advice on genre standards, market positioning, and any pre-publishing needs like editing.

2

Formatting & Interior Design

We professionally format your manuscript for print and eBook (e.g., margins, fonts, chapter headings, table of contents, page numbers). We ensure compliance with platform requirements (Amazon KDP, IngramSpark) and create a clean, reader-friendly interior layout.

3

Cover Design & Branding

We create eye-catching, market-appropriate covers (front, back, spine) that reflect your book's genre and appeal to readers. We incorporate your title, author name, and any imagery, ensuring high-resolution files for print and digital.

4

ISBN & Metadata Setup

We assign ISBNs (free or premium), set up metadata (keywords, categories, description), and prepare files for platforms like Amazon KDP, Ingram, Okadabooks, and others. This ensures maximum discoverability and sales potential.

5

Distribution & Launch Support

We upload to global print-on-demand and eBook platforms, set pricing, and provide guidance on marketing, launch strategies, and royalties tracking. You receive full access to sales dashboards and ongoing support.
