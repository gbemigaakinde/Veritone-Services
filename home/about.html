Our Story

Founded in Nigeria by seasoned communicators and published authors, Veritone Services was born from a simple belief: powerful words can transform ideas into influence. We saw a gap in premium, discreet writing services for high-caliber professionals, CEOs, politicians, and brands.

Today, we serve clients across Africa and internationally, delivering ghostwriting, editing, publishing, and strategic communications with unwavering excellence and confidentiality.

Our name, Veritone, reflects our commitment to truth (veritas) and tone—the essence of every message we craft.
